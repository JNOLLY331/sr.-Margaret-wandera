Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Sr. Margaret's academic background?

Sr. Margaret holds a PhD from Maseno University and has extensive experience in academic research. She is currently a lecturer at the Technical University of Kenya.

Where has Sr. Margaret served in her religious life?

She has served in various parishes including St. Teresa Parish Eastleigh in Nairobi, Bura Mission in Taita Taveta, Voi, Makupa, and currently at Consolata Shrines Westlands in Nairobi.

When did Sr. Margaret take her vows?

She took her first profession on December 8, 1995, and her perpetual vows on August 15, 2004. She celebrated her Silver Jubilee on August 12, 2022.

How can I learn more about Sr. Margaret's work?

You can find more information about her academic publications through her ORCID profile at https://orcid.org/0000-0002-6805-5812 and by visiting her current institution's website.
